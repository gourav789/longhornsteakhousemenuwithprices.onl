Prices vary by location and are subject to change without notice. We make every effort to keep our information up to date, but for guaranteed accurate pricing, please check directly with your local restaurant or the official LongHorn Steakhouse website.

We review and update our menu content regularly — typically whenever we become aware of new items, price changes, or discontinued dishes. 💡 Quick Tips for Your LongHorn Visit

While you're here, here are a few helpful tips we've gathered for making the most of your LongHorn Steakhouse dining experience:

  • Arrive Early for Happy Hour — LongHorn typically offers happy hour specials on cocktails and appetizers during early evening hours. Check with your local restaurant for specific times and offerings.
  • Try the Lunch Menu — Available Monday through Saturday until 3:00 PM, the lunch menu offers many of the same great flavors in slightly smaller portions at a lower price point. Great for a weekday treat!
  • Ask About Seasonal Specials — LongHorn regularly rotates seasonal and limited-time offerings that don't always appear on the standard menu. Your server will know exactly what's available.
  • The Chocolate Stampede is Shareable — At ~1,500 calories and a generous size, this legendary dessert is designed for 2–3 people. Perfect for sharing after a big steak dinner!
  • Customize Your Steak — LongHorn's kitchen will season your steak exactly how you like it, and you can add toppings like mushrooms, onions, or cheese. Don't be afraid to ask.
  • Check for Online Waitlists — Many LongHorn locations now offer digital waitlists through the official app, so you can check in remotely and reduce your wait time during busy hours.
  • Kids Eat Free Deals — Certain locations offer kids' dining promotions on specific days. Contact your local restaurant to find out if they participate.
