About Us

Your ultimate unofficial guide to everything LongHorn Steakhouse.

Who We Are

Welcome to longhornsteakhousemenuwithprices.onl! We are a dedicated team of steak lovers, food enthusiasts, and bargain hunters who wanted to create the most comprehensive, easy-to-use, and up-to-date guide for LongHorn Steakhouse fans across the country.

Like many of you, we love the bold flavors and perfectly grilled steaks at LongHorn. However, we often found ourselves struggling to find accurate, up-to-date menu prices and complete nutritional information before deciding where to eat. We realized there was a need for a clean, fast, and reliable platform that brings all this information together in one place. That's how this website was born.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide you with a clean, user-friendly platform where you can easily browse the complete LongHorn Steakhouse menu, check approximate prices, and explore nutritional information before you dine. We believe that informed diners have better dining experiences.

Whether you're planning a family dinner, a romantic date night, or a quick lunch with coworkers, we want to help you plan your meal and budget accordingly so there are no surprises when the check arrives.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Menus: We cover everything from their legendary fire-grilled steaks and signature sides to irresistible desserts and handcrafted cocktails.
  • Accurate Price Estimates: We constantly track and update approximate prices to help you plan your meal budget effectively.
  • Nutritional Insights: Making healthy choices is important, so we aim to provide caloric guidelines and nutritional overviews where possible.
  • Location Information: Need to find the nearest LongHorn Steakhouse? We've got resources to point you in the right direction.
  • Dining Tips & Tricks: We share insider tips on happy hours, lunch specials, and how to get the most value out of your visit.

How We Source Our Information

We gather our information through a combination of crowd-sourced reports from diners across different states, public menu data, and independent research. Because prices can vary significantly depending on local taxes, regional wages, and specific franchise pricing models, we provide price ranges or median estimates that represent a typical dining experience.
