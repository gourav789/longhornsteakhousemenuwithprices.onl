  • We only collect personal data when you voluntarily contact us through our contact form.
  • We use basic analytics tools to understand website traffic — this data is anonymized and aggregated.
  • We never sell your personal information to third parties, ever.
  • You have the right to request deletion of any personal data we hold about you.
  • We use cookies only for essential site functionality and anonymous analytics.

For the full details, please read the complete policy below. If you have any questions, contact us at any time.

1. Introduction & Identity of the Data Controller

Welcome to longhornsteakhousemenuwithprices.onl (referred to herein as "the Website," "we," "us," or "our"). We are an independent informational website that provides menu information, pricing guides, and location details relating to LongHorn Steakhouse restaurants in the United States.

We are committed to protecting your personal information and your fundamental right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains in full detail:

  • What personal information we collect from you and why
  • How we use and store that information
  • How long we retain your data
  • What rights you have over your personal data
  • How to exercise those rights
  • How to contact us with privacy concerns

By using our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, you should discontinue use of our website immediately.

Important clarification: This website is NOT affiliated with LongHorn Steakhouse or Darden Restaurants, Inc. We are an independent third-party publisher. For LongHorn Steakhouse's own privacy policy, please visit longhornsteakhouse.com.

2. What Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Voluntarily Provide

We only collect personal information when you choose to give it to us. This happens when you use our Contact form. The information we collect in this case includes:

  • Full name — To address you properly in our response
  • Email address — To send you our reply to your inquiry
  • Subject/reason for contact — To route your inquiry to the appropriate team member
  • Message content — The information you include in your message
  • Menu item reference — If you are reporting a specific error (optional field)

We do not require you to create an account, register, or provide personal information to browse any part of our website. All menu information, pricing, and location content is freely accessible without providing any personal data.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain technical information is automatically collected by your browser and our hosting infrastructure. This includes:

  • IP address — Automatically logged by our hosting server (GitHub Pages / Cloudflare), typically anonymized or truncated
  • Browser type and version — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc., and their version numbers
  • Operating system — Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, etc.
  • Device type — Desktop, tablet, or mobile device
  • Screen resolution — Used to analyze how the site renders on different devices
  • Pages visited — Which pages of our website you viewed and in what order
  • Time spent on each page — How long you remained on each page
  • Referring URL — Which website or search engine directed you to our site
  • Date and time of access — When you visited our site
  • Search terms used — If you found us through a search engine, the search query used (where available)

This automatically collected data is used solely to understand how our website is being used so we can improve it. It is never linked to any personally identifiable information and is processed only in aggregated, anonymized form.

2.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website uses cookies — small text files that are stored on your device when you visit certain websites. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential for the website to function properly. Without these cookies, some features of our site may not work as intended. These cookies do not collect any personally identifiable information.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use Google Analytics or similar services to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect data in anonymized, aggregated form — we cannot identify individual users through this data. Information collected includes pages viewed, time on site, bounce rate, and traffic sources.
  • Preference Cookies: If implemented, these cookies remember your preferences (such as which menu tab you last had open) to improve your experience on return visits.

You have the right to control cookies through your browser settings. Most modern browsers allow you to view, manage, block, and delete cookies. Note that blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. For more information about managing cookies, visit aboutcookies.org.

3. How We Use Your Information

We are committed to using your personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected. Here is a full breakdown of how we use each type of data:

3.1 Contact Form Data

  • To respond to your inquiry: Your name, email, and message content are used exclusively to prepare and send a reply to your question, report, or feedback.
  • To improve our content: If you report a menu error, we use that information to research and potentially update our content (without linking the correction to your identity).
  • For spam and fraud prevention: We may use your email address to identify and block repeated spam or abusive submissions.

3.2 Analytics Data

  • Website performance analysis: We use anonymized traffic data to understand which pages are most visited, where users come from, and how they navigate our site.
  • Content improvement: Analytics help us identify which menu categories are most popular, which pages have high bounce rates, and where users seem to get confused — allowing us to improve the site experience.
  • Technical issue identification: Traffic patterns can help us detect and investigate technical problems.

3.3 Legal Obligations

In rare circumstances, we may be required to use or disclose your personal information to comply with a legal obligation, court order, or valid governmental request. In such cases, we will disclose only the minimum amount of data required by law.

What we will NEVER do with your data:

  • Sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal information to any third party
  • Use your information for unsolicited marketing or advertising
  • Share your data with advertisers for targeted advertising purposes
  • Use your data to build a personal profile without your explicit consent

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data only where we have a valid legal basis to do so under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our legal bases are:

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): When you submit our contact form, you are giving us your express consent to process your personal data for the purpose of responding to your message. You may withdraw this consent at any time.
  • Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f)): We use analytics data to understand how our website is used and improve it. This is in the legitimate interest of both us (as website operators) and our users (who benefit from an improved experience).
  • Legal Obligation (Article 6(1)(c)): In cases where we are required by law to retain or disclose data.

5. Third-Party Services We Use

Our website works with several third-party services that may collect data independently. We are transparent about all of them:

5.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (a service provided by Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA) to analyze website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics places cookies on your device and sends anonymized data to Google's servers. We have enabled IP anonymization, meaning your full IP address is never stored. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts (Google LLC) to provide a premium reading experience. When your browser loads a font, Google may log the request, including your IP address and browser information. We use Google Fonts via a direct link (not local self-hosting). Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service provided by GitHub, Inc. (subsidiary of Microsoft Corporation). GitHub Pages automatically logs certain server-side data, including IP addresses and request logs, as part of standard hosting operations. GitHub's Privacy Statement: GitHub Privacy Statement.

5.4 Cloudflare (CDN & Security)

We may use Cloudflare (Cloudflare, Inc., 101 Townsend St., San Francisco, CA 94107) for content delivery and security protection. Cloudflare processes network traffic for security and performance purposes and may store access logs for a limited period. Cloudflare's Privacy Policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain personal data longer than necessary for the purposes for which it was collected. Our specific retention periods are:

  • Contact form submissions: Retained for a maximum of 12 months from the date of submission. After this period, messages and all associated personal data are permanently deleted from our systems.
  • Google Analytics data: Retained for 26 months in anonymized, aggregated form, as per Google Analytics default settings. No personally identifiable data is retained in Analytics.
  • Server access logs (GitHub Pages / Cloudflare): Retained per the respective service provider's policies — typically 30–90 days before automatic deletion.

After the applicable retention period, all personal data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. You may request early deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 9).

7. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, accidental loss, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Our security measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our website is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology, indicated by the padlock icon in your browser's address bar.
  • Hosting security: Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages / Cloudflare) maintains industry-standard security infrastructure, including DDoS protection and access controls.
  • Minimal data collection: We only collect the minimum data necessary — reducing the risk and impact of any potential data incident.
  • Access restrictions: Only authorized personnel have access to any personal data submitted through our contact form.

However, please be aware that no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information using commercially acceptable means, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have reason to believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have significant rights regarding your personal data. We are committed to honoring all of these rights:

For All Users:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to know what personal data we hold about you, how we collected it, and how we use it.
  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you at any time. We will provide this within 30 days of your request.
  • Right to Rectification: If any personal data we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request that we correct it.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request that we permanently delete all personal data we hold about you. We will comply within 30 days unless we have a legal obligation to retain certain data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If we are processing your data based on your consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time. This does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

For EEA / UK Users (GDPR):

  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You can request that we restrict how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format and to transfer it to another data controller.
  • Right to Object: You can object to our processing of your personal data where we rely on legitimate interests as our legal basis.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection supervisory authority if you believe we have not handled your data in compliance with applicable law.

For California Residents (CCPA / CPRA):

  • Right to Know What Personal Information is Collected: As described in Section 2 of this policy.
  • Right to Know Whether Personal Information is Sold or Disclosed: We do NOT sell personal information. We do NOT disclose personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: Not applicable — we do not sell personal data.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.
  • Right to Delete Personal Information: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. See Section 9 to exercise this right.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@longhornsteakhousemenuwithprices.onl or use our Contact page. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days. In complex cases, we may extend this period by a further 60 days, in which case we will notify you.
